Legacy & Excellence Since 1980

Our Institution

Established in 1980 under the visionary leadership of Late Shri. K. H. Patil ji, Rural Engineering College (REC), Hulkoti is a premier institution dedicated to transforming rural youth into global tech leaders.

As a part of the esteemed R.T.E. Society, our college was founded with the blessings of Sri Sri Sri Thiruchi Mahaswamiji. Over 45 years, we have evolved into a NAAC Accredited and UGC Recognized hub of academic excellence.

Innovation Unlocked

RECKON 1.O Hackathon

RECKON 1.O is the flagship national-level 24-hour hackathon hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering. It serves as a playground for developers, designers, and innovators to transform wild ideas into working prototypes.

Our goal is to foster a culture of rapid building and collaborative problem-solving. Whether it's tackling climate change or building the next big thing in AI, RECKON 1.O provides the platform, the mentorship, and the community to make it happen.

Why RECKON 1.O?

  • 24 Hours of Intensive Coding
  • Expert Mentorship from Leading Industries
  • Networking with like-minded Innovators
  • Exciting Prizes and Recognition
