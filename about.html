About Our Institution

Established in 1980 by the R.T.E. Society, Rural Engineering College (REC), Hulkoti is a premier engineering institution dedicated to bringing world-class technical education to rural Karnataka.

With over four decades of academic legacy, REC Hulkoti has been a beacon of innovation, recognized by **UGC** and accredited by **NAAC**. We pride ourselves on creating engineers who don't just solve problems but redefine them.

Innovation Unlocked

REC 1.O Hackathon

REC 1.O is the flagship national-level 24-hour hackathon hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering. It serves as a playground for developers, designers, and innovators to transform wild ideas into working prototypes.

Our goal is to foster a culture of rapid building and collaborative problem-solving. Whether it's tackling climate change or building the next big thing in AI, REC 1.O provides the platform, the mentorship, and the community to make it happen.

Why REC 1.O?

  • 24 Hours of Intensive Coding
  • Expert Mentorship from Leading Industries
  • Networking with like-minded Innovators
  • Exciting Prizes and Recognition
