What is REC 1.O?

REC 1.O is an open 24-hour inter-college hackathon welcoming student innovators from any college or university. Organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Rural Engineering College, Hulkoti.

Form your team, pick your theme, and spend 24 non-stop hours building real solutions — with expert mentors, industry leaders, and fellow innovators by your side.

Event Timeline

🕐 Registration & Check-In

🚀 Hackathon Begins!
